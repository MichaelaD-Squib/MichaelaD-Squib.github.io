About Me

I am a Harm Reduction Educator and Advocate. In 2019, I created a non-profit harm reduction organization called “Safe Raving”.

Payton Govas,Jessica Good and myself, provide non-judgmental, pragmatic, and compassionate support to our community and the clients we serve together.
We provide harm reduction support and services to clients at raves and festivals.
An example of our services includes how we teach people about ORRP.
This is the 3rd year Safe Raving has been active.

More About Me
